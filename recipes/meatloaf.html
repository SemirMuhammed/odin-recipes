Easy Meatloaf Recipe

Description

This meatloaf recipe doesn't take long to make at all, and it's very good!

Get back to basics with this easy meatloaf recipe. It's quick, traditional, delicious, and it's sure to please everyone at your table.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ poinds ground beef
  • 1 egg
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup dried bread crumbs
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • ⅓ cup ketchup
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons prepared mustard

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Lightly grease a 9x5-inch loaf pan.
  3. Combine ground beef, onion, milk, bread crumbs and egg in a large bowl.
  4. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Transfer into prepared loaf pan.
  6. Mix ketchup, brown sugar, and mustard together in a small bowl until well combined.
  7. Pour over meatloaf and spread it evenly over the top.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven until no longer pink in the center, about 1 hour.
